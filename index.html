Karnataka and Uttarakhand Cooking Without Fire Competition

About Karnataka

Karnataka is known for its rich culture, coffee plantations, Mysore Palace, and diverse traditional foods like chaat, millet-based dishes, and refreshing drinks.

About Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand, known as the “Land of the Gods”, is famous for the Himalayas, apples, walnuts, and healthy traditional hill cuisine.

🥗 Nutritional Information

Here is the approximate nutritional breakdown of the foods featured on this website.

🥗 Congress Chaat

Calories: 250–350 kcal (per serving)

Main nutrients: Carbohydrates, Fiber, Small amount of protein

Health note: Contains chutneys and spices; can be moderately high in calories due to fried base.

🍛 Bonda

Calories: 200–300 kcal (per 2 pieces)

Main nutrients: Carbohydrates, Fat, Small protein

Health note: Deep-fried snack, best eaten occasionally.

🍎 Apple Mocktail

Calories: 80–150 kcal (per glass)

Main nutrients: Natural sugars, Vitamin C

Health note: Healthier drink option, especially if low sugar is used.

🥨 Himalayan Nachos

Calories: 300–450 kcal (per serving)

Main nutrients: Carbohydrates, Fat, Sodium

Health note: High in salt and cheese depending on toppings.

🥥 Coconut Laddoo

Calories: 120–180 kcal (per piece)

Main nutrients: Healthy fats, Carbohydrates

Health note: Natural sweet made with coconut, but contains sugar.

🍫 Chocolate Walnut Truffles

Calories: 150–250 kcal (per piece)

Main nutrients: Fats, Sugar, Antioxidants (from cocoa), Protein (from walnuts)

Health note: Rich dessert, best in moderation.
